This Privacy Policy describes how Tyrant Team 7 Spa collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application Tiles In Time, available on the Google Play Store.

By downloading and using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We collect different types of information to enhance the gaming experience and maintain application functionality. When you use the Application, we may automatically collect certain information, which includes, but is not limited to:

  • Mobile device type and manufacturer.
  • Device operating system (e.g., Android 12).

  • IP address.
  • General location data (city/country level), if required by the application.
  • Crash and performance data (Crash logs).

The Application utilizes carefully selected third-party services that may collect and process information used to identify you. Data shared with these services is limited to what is necessary for their function.

Below, we list the service providers we use and their respective privacy policies:

3. Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and optimize the operation of the game.
  • Notify you about changes to our application or services.
  • Allow you to participate in interactive features (such as multiplayer) when you choose to do so.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Gather analytics and statistics: We use industry-standard analytics services (including Google Firebase Analytics) to better understand game usage, performance, and user preferences, which allows us to identify areas for improvement and optimize game design.
  • Monitor service usage and detect, prevent, or resolve technical issues.

4. Data Retention

We will retain your automatically collected Information only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. This generally means:

  • Crash and performance data (Crash logs): Retained for up to 90 days for diagnostic purposes and deleted after technical issues are resolved.
  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID) and analytical data: Retained for a maximum period of 26 months before being anonymized or aggregated, for the purpose of long-term trend analysis.
  • Other data will be deleted or anonymized when it is no longer relevant to the operation and improvement of the application.

5. Children's Privacy

We comply with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play Families guidelines. We do not collect precise personal information from children, nor do we display personalized advertisements (behavioral advertising) to users identified as under 13 years of age.

If you become aware that a child under your care has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take the necessary steps to remove such information.

6. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us with your information, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect it. Our practices include:

  • Encryption: Communication data between your device and our servers is transmitted using standard security protocols (HTTPS/SSL).
  • Anonymization and Aggregation: Whenever possible, information is processed anonymously or in aggregated form before analysis.
  • Restricted Access: Access to raw data is strictly limited to personnel who require it for the purposes described in this policy.

However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
